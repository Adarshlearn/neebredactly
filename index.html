Clinical Trial Redaction
📋
CTR 536/2014 · EMA Policy 0070 · Revised CTIS Rules (June 2024) — Applies minimum-redaction principle. Deferral mechanism removed. Sponsors must submit publication-ready documents. Structured data fields cannot be redacted; functional institutional contacts must not be redacted.

🇺🇸 ClinicalTrials.gov
Clinical Trial Redaction
📋
42 CFR Part 11 · FDAAA · HIPAA Safe Harbor (45 CFR §164.514(b)) — All 18 HIPAA identifiers must be removed. State-level geographic data is generally acceptable. Protocols and SAPs must be disclosed. Results submitted within 12 months of primary completion date.

🇨🇦 Health Canada PRCI
Clinical Trial Redaction
📋
PRCI 2019 · Privacy Act · Food and Drugs Act — Prefers generalization over redaction for indirect identifiers. Subject IDs should be pseudonymized (not deleted) to preserve data linkage. Sponsors have 60 days from HC notification to submit de-identified documents plus anonymization report.
